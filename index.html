Hello!

This is a script I wrote to allow Twitch to control the player of the Choice Chamber game. Click the character at the top to learn more about the game.

Head here to see not only the code for the script, but also the website!

You may also be here for troubleshooting, and in that case, head here to see that.

Player Commands:
jump
jump makes player 1 jump
attack makes player 1 attack
jumpattack makes player 1 attack in mid-air
doublejumpattack makes player 1 attack in mid-air and double jump
left makes player 1 move left
hardleft makes player 1 REALLY move left
jumpleft makes player 1 move left and jump
jumphardleft makes player 1 REALLY move left and jump
right makes player 1 move right
hardright makes player 1 REALLY move right
jumpright makes player 1 move right and jump
jumphardright makes player 1 REALLY move right and jump
p2 toggles player 2
p2left makes player 2 move left
p2hardleft makes player 2 REALLY move left
p2right makes player 2 move right
p2hardright makes player 2 REALLY move right
p2up makes player 2 move up
p2hardup makes player 2 REALLY move up
p2up makes player 2 move up
p2hardup makes player 2 REALLY move up

Event Commands:
!ccstats sends a summary of the game's events to the chat
!cc sends message within game
mods only. must be enabled in options
